About us

What is HeartCode:

nitiative by Ellipsis, in collaboration with Devera to expose underprivileged youths to programming and computer science concepts.


Vision:

To create a sustainable framework for teaching underprivileged students, to be implemented nationwide


Mission:

That through our program, a passion for programming and problem solving will be ignited within these youths.
