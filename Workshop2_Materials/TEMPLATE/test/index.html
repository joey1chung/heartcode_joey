About us

What is HeartCode:

Initiative by Ellipsis, in collaboration with Devera to expose underprivileged youths to programming and computer science concepts.

Vision:

To create a sustainable framework for teaching underprivileged youths, to be implemented nation wide

Mission:

That through our programme, a pasion for programming and problem solving will be ignited within these youths
